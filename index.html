Rules of the Game

Just like tic tac toe, this game is played between two players, adding a symbol to the board in turns. However, unlike tic tac toe, the players use the same symbol (a cross), and can both contribute to the same line. Interestingly, although this is a simplification of tic tac toe, it actually improves the game by making a draw impossible instead of practically guaranteed. Note that in the game below, each player's symbol is drawn in the same color.

How to Play?

In this multiplayer game, you have two options: "Create Game" and "Join Game." To begin, you need to provide your name and make selections for the rows, columns, and winning condition. The number of rows and columns determines the dimensions of the grid, while the winning condition length specifies the number of consecutive symbols required to win (e.g., making a line of three 'X' marks).

After entering your name and selecting the game parameters, you can press the "Create Game" button. This action will generate a unique Room ID that you can share with your friend to invite them to join the game.

To join a game, your friend needs to input their name and request the Room ID from you. Once they have the Room ID, they can choose the "Join Game" option.
